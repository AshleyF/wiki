## 07 JUL 2026 Tue - Vegas Day 9 - Meow Wolf

We had breakfast at Leone Cafe and lunch at Jersey Mike's. We went to this crazy place in Area 15 called Meow Wolf. You start in this "Omega Mart" grocery store and end up finding all these secret passages to other areas. It's actually a multi-story place five times bigger than Omega Mart. All kinds of absurd things. Part art exhibit, part escape room, part fever dream, all wrapped in a mystery to unravel. It was a lot of fun. We could probably spend *days* there before seeing everything and solving the puzzles.
