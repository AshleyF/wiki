# Drum Rudiments

The [40 essential drum rudiments](https://ae.vicfirth.com/education/40-essential-rudiments/).

## Rolls

### Single Stroke Roll

```drums
tempo 100
meter 4/4
division 16
sn:    x x x x x x x x x x x x x x x x
stick: R L R L R L R L R L R L R L R L
```

### Single Stroke Four

```drums
tempo 100
meter 4/4
division 12
sn:    x x x x> . . x x x x> . .
stick: R L R L  . . L R L R  . .
```

### Single Stroke Seven

```drums
tempo 100
meter 4/4
division 24
sn:    x x x x x x x> . . . . . x x x x x x x> . . . . .
stick: R L R L R L R  . . . . . L R L R L R L  . . . . .
```

### Multiple Bounce Roll

```drums
tempo 100
meter 4/4
division 16
sn:    x/// x/// x/// x/// x/// x/// x/// x/// x/// x/// x/// x/// x/// x/// x/// x///
stick: R    L    R    L    R    L    R    L    R    L    R    L    R    L    R    L
```

### Triple Stroke Roll

```drums
tempo 100
meter 4/4
division 12
sn:    x x x x x x x x x x x x
stick: R R R L L L R R R L L L
```
